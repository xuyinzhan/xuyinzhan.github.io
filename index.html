Yinzhan Xu

I'm a postdoc in the Computer Science department at UCSD, hosted by Barna Saha . Previously, I completed my PhD at MIT, where I was lucky to be advised by Virginia Vassilevska Williams. Prior to that, I obtained a B.S. degree in Computer Science and Math from MIT.

I have a broad interest in theoretical computer science, with special interests in fine-grained complexity and algorithms.

You can contact me at xyzhan at mit dot edu.
